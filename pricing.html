Support & Pricing

Free for individuals.
Supported by businesses.

Gledhill Metadata is open source and free for everyone to use. Businesses are encouraged to contribute based on the value they receive.

How it works

✨ Free for individuals

Students, hobbyists, freelancers, and anyone learning photo metadata work can use Gledhill Metadata completely free, forever. No limits, no tracking.

🤝 Honor-based for businesses

Organizations that use Gledhill Metadata for production work, training, or integration are encouraged to contribute. It's completely voluntary — there's no enforcement or licensing check.
